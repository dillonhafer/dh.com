Pandora's Box

Prop Open
    How do I use it?
    • Install the extension from the chrome store
    • And your finished! Pandora will always play while a tab is open.
    Remember: you can pause or play the stream by clicking on the url icon.
Why have it?

I wanted to be able to listen to Pandora around the house without having to keep clicking a link every hour, so I wrote this extension for Chrome that "props" open Pandora Box. It should be called "Pandora's Box Cutter". Feel free to email me a more creative name.

Enjoy! :)
