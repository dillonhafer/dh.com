HowToLogSudo¶

Add the following to lines to /etc/sudoers

Defaults mailto="jackc@hylesanderson.edu" 
Defaults mail_always

Remember to use visudo to edit.

Note something really weird. mail_always causes a 

sudo su
to log in and immediately log out. The more correct way to get another user's shell is 
sudo -s
. The correct way works, but for some reason 
sudo su
doesn't.
